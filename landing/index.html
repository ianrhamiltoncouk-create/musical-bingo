How Patrons Play

When players scan your custom QR code on their tables, they get a gorgeous neon card displaying 9 random tracks from your playlist. As the host plays music, players listen and daub called tracks.

The moment someone hits a line or full house, the system alerts the host and triggers screen fireworks. Patrons never have to shout or wait—wins are verified instantly.
